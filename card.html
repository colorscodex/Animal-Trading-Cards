Tiger

The pattern of the stripes is unique to each specimen, it is a bar code!
  • Habitat: Asia
  • Healthy habits: The tigers like water
  • Diet: Varied
  • Special characteristics: Vision of color

The tigers like water; they are a good swimmer and enjoy with a good water bath. They are the largest of the cats and like them, they have a certain vision of color. Its pelage is orange crossed by stripes of shades ranging from dark brown to black. The shape and quantity of the stripes vary according to sex Tigers are beautiful and very special.
